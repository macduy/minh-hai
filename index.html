    Soviet Union 1980s

Travel

Public transport

  • Coach from London: Oxford Tube, 2h
  • Train from London: ~1h

From the airport

We recommend landing at Heathrow or Gatwick, from where you can take The Airline bus directly to Oxford.

  • Heathrow: 1.5 hours, ~£30 return
  • Gatwick: 2 hours, ~£37 return

From Luton or Stansted, you can take a National Express coach. 3-4 hours, ~£25 if booked early.

In Oxford

To get to Lady Margaret Hall:

  • Taxi: 10 minutes, ~£7. No Uber.
  • Bus: Take 14 to Bevington Road, then 6 mins walk down Norham Gardens.

Driving

  • 1.5-2.5 hours from center of London, depending on traffic
  • Parking space is available at the college but is limited. Please let us know in your RSVP if you would like to use it.

Accommodation

Unfortunately Oxford can be an expensive place to stay. However, if you would like to stay the night after the festivities, perhaps to explore the city the next morning, there are a few options.

  • Lina Guest House: Close to Lady Margaret Hall, prices from £100
  • Five Mile Oxford: Slightly further out but still in town, prices from £80
  • We can also try put you up with some friends in Oxford.

We will update this section as we find more hotels and secure special deals.
